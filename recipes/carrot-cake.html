Carrot Cake Recipe

Description

Carrot cake is a moist, dense spice cake where grated carrots are incorporated into the batter, serving not just as a flavor component but primarily to ensure a tender and moist texture. The carrots natural sweetness allows for a moderate amount of added sugar, and the cake's flavor profile is typically dominated by warm spices such as cinnamon, nutmeg, and sometimes ginger or allspice The base ingredients usually include flour, eggs, sugar, vegetable oil (which further enhances moisture), and baking soda. Common optional add-ins that contribute are pecans, raisins, or shredded coconut.

The definitive finishing touch that completes the classic carrot cake experience is a thick layer of sweet, tangy cream cheese frosting. This smooth, rich topping provides a perfect balance to the spiced, dense crumb of the cake itself. While its exact origins are debated, modern carrot cake gained significant popularity in the United States during the food rationing periods of World War II, when home bakers used the readily available and naturally sweet root vegetable as a sugar substitute. It remains a beloved dessert, cherished for its unique combination of texture, spice, and creaminess.

Dry Ingredients

  • Flour.
  • baking powder.
  • Sugar.
  • Powdered sugar.
  • Salt.
  • Cinnamon.
  • Nutmeg
  • Walnuts(optional).
  • Raisins(optional)

Wet ingredients

  • Eggs.
  • Vegetable oil.
  • Butter.
  • Cream cheese.
  • Vanilla extract.

Cake cooking instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch pan, or two 9-inch round cake pans, and line the bottoms with parchment paper.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt until well combined.
  3. Mix Wet Ingredients In a separate large bowl, whisk the oil, eggs, and vanilla extract until smooth and well combined.
  4. Combine Pour the wet ingredients into the dry mixture. Stir until just combined, being careful not to overmix the batter. A few streaks of flour may remain.
  5. Fold in Add-ins Gently fold in the grated carrots and chopped nuts (if using) using a spatula.
  6. Bake, Pour the batter evenly into your prepared pan and bake for 35-45 minutes.
  7. Test if the cake is done with a toothpick or cake tester inserted into the center, if it comes out clean or with a few moist crumbs that indicates a perfectly done cake
  8. Cool Let the cake layers cool in the pans for 10-15 minutes before inverting them onto a wire rack to cool completely. The cake must be completely cool before frosting.

Cream cheese frosting instructions

  1. Beat Fats In a large bowl using an electric mixer, beat the softened cream cheese and softened butter together until smooth and fluffy.
  2. Add Sugar and Vanilla Gradually add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract, beating on low speed until the frosting is smooth and spreadable
  3. Spread the cream cheese frosting evenly over the top of the cooled cake.
  4. And finally, serve! For cleaner slices chill the cake for at least 30 minutes before cutting and serving, enjoy!
